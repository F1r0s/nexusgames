1. Information We Collect

When you use Mod Vault, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, search queries, and game cards accessed.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and IP address for regional offer matching.
  • Support Data: Name and email address submitted via our support contact form.
  • Local Storage: We store a local game database cache on your device to speed up page loads. This expires every 5 minutes.

We do not require account registration and do not collect passwords or payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Deliver the correct regional offers and content via the locker system.
  • Improve site performance and fix technical issues.
  • Respond to support requests submitted by you.
  • Generate aggregate, anonymised analytics to understand user behavior.
We do not sell your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

3. Website Improvement Tasks

To keep Mod Vault free and constantly improving, we occasionally ask users to complete a short quick task before accessing a mod — such as trying out a partner app, answering a quick survey, or signing up for a free trial.

Think of it like a "help us, help you" system: completing one small task funds server costs, content updates, and new features — so the library keeps growing at zero cost to you.

Important: When you click on a task, you are briefly redirected to a partner page. That partner page has its own privacy policy. Mod Vault does not receive or store any personal data from those pages.

Completing a task is tracked only to verify your access for that session. Once confirmed, your download unlocks immediately.

🔒 Your data stays local. Any information collected during a task stays on your device or with the partner service. We do not receive, store, or sell any of your personal data from these tasks — to anyone, ever.

If you ever encounter a task that feels misleading or uncomfortable, please contact our support team and we will remove it immediately.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use the following tracking technologies:

  • Google Analytics (gtag.js): Collects anonymised usage data including page views, session duration, and device type. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Records anonymised session replays and heatmaps to help us improve the user interface. No personally identifiable data is captured.
  • localStorage: Used to cache the game database locally for performance. No personal data is stored here.

Third-party advertisers served through the offer wall may set their own cookies when you click on an offer. Mod Vault has no control over these cookies.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Improvement Task Partners: Your IP address and browser type are shared with our task partner to identify which tasks are available in your region. No name, email, or personal profile is ever transmitted.
  • Analytics Providers: Anonymised usage data is shared with Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of any personal data tied to your support submissions.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of analytics tracking via your browser settings or the Google Analytics opt-out tool.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.

To exercise these rights, contact us via the Support page.

7. Children's Privacy

Mod Vault is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact our support team and we will delete it promptly.
